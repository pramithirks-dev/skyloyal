Why fly with us

Three things we promise on every flight.

The way Ireland's first generation of network airlines did it. With discipline, with care, and with the modern aircraft that make both possible.

✈

Direct, when it matters

Every long-haul we operate is direct. No second-airport changes in winter. No surprise layovers built into the lowest fare to make it cheaper on paper.

⚖

Transparent fares

The price you see on the search page is the price you pay at booking. Bag fees, seat fees, and tax are shown up front, not at the last screen.

🌱

Sustainable fuel, real numbers

We publish the percentage of Sustainable Aviation Fuel on every flight, every quarter. Currently 11%. We are working to 30% by 2030.
