How it works

Three things we do, every time you fly.

SkyLoyal turns the way you fly into rewards built around you. In plain English, here is how.

1 We notice the journey
Behind the scenes

The flights you take. The miles you earn. The destinations you choose. Quietly, with your consent, so when we get in touch we have something useful to share.

2 We get in touch
When it counts

Every email tells you why you got it, and a Customer Success Manager reviews every personalised offer before it leaves our team. Real human review, every time.

3 You decide
Always your call

Take the offer, change the cadence, or skip it. Every "surprise" is a real benefit already loaded to your wallet. No fake countdowns. No pressure. Ever.
